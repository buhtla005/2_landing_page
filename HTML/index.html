What is Photography?

Photography is art, an application for practice and creating images by emulating recording light or other electromagnetic radiation, either electronically by means of an image sensor. It is employed in many fields of science, manufacturing (e.g., photolithography), and business, as well as its more direct uses for art, film and video production, recreational purposes, hobby, and mass communication.

It is state of the art app built for end users to upload enjoy and evaluate other works of talented artists.
